About

Who we are

Welcome to Yuvaanand, a beacon of hope and change in the heart of rural India. Established with a vision of uplifting the lives of those who reside in our villages, we are a non-governmental organization driven by a passion for creating a brighter and more prosperous future for all.

Yuvaanand is more than just an organization; it's a movement driven by the belief that together, we can create a brighter tomorrow for rural India. Join us in our mission to empower, inspire, and transform lives one village at a time.

At Yuvaanand, we believe that every individual, regardless of their geographical location, deserves access to the opportunities and facilities that can improve their quality of life. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower rural communities by providing essential facilities and fostering skill development.

About Us

Our Story

Yuvaanand was founded by a group of dedicated individuals who shared a common dream: to bridge the gap between rural and urban India. We recognized the challenges faced by our fellow citizens in rural areas, from the lack of basic amenities to limited access to skill-building opportunities. Determined to make a difference, we embarked on this journey to create lasting change.

About Us

Training Programs

Training programs run by "Yuva Anand Sarvajan Kalyan Samiti" are organized with the aim of promoting skill development of youth. Through these training programs youth study various skills, such as computer education, technical training, agriculture , Industrial Training, Business Training, and other areas.These training programs help the youth to prepare for employment and help them find employment opportunities as per their skills. This committee contributes to social welfare by conducting these training programs to provide economic empowerment to the youth.

About Us

Mission

Our mission is to empower rural communities with the knowledge, resources, and skills they need to create a brighter and more prosperous future. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between urban and rural India by addressing the unique challenges faced by those in remote areas.

Our commitment extends to:
  • Promoting Skill Development :
    We aim to equip the youth in rural communities with essential skills for employment and entrepreneurship, fostering self-reliance and economic independence.
  • Facilitating Access to Essential Facilities :
    We work to ensure that every village has access to crucial facilities such as electricity, health services, and education, laying the foundation for growth and development.
  • Creating Awareness :
    We strive to educate and inform rural citizens about government programs, health practices, and modern agricultural techniques, empowering them to make informed decisions about their lives.
  • Celebrating Culture and Unity :
    We celebrate the rich cultural heritage of our villages and foster a sense of unity by organizing events and programs that bring communities together.
  • Empowering Individuals and Communities :
    Through our initiatives, we encourage individuals to take charge of their own lives, promote collective development, and inspire hope for a better tomorrow.
