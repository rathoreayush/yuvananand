About

Who we are

Welcome to Yuvaanand, a beacon of hope and change in the heart of rural India. Established with a vision of uplifting the lives of those who reside in our villages, we are a non-governmental organization driven by a passion for creating a brighter and more prosperous future for all.

Yuvaanand is more than just an organization; it's a movement driven by the belief that together, we can create a brighter tomorrow for rural India. Join us in our mission to empower, inspire, and transform lives one village at a time.

At Yuvaanand, we believe that every individual, regardless of their geographical location, deserves access to the opportunities and facilities that can improve their quality of life. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower rural communities by providing essential facilities and fostering skill development.

Working Area

Programs and Initiatives

Electricity for Agriculture

To provide farmers with reliable electricity solutions for enhanced agricultural productivity.

Skill India

To equip the youth in rural communities with essential skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

Skill Development Training

To facilitate access to high-quality skill development training programs for students in rural areas.

Government Program Awareness

To spread awareness about government programs and schemes that can positively impact rural lives.

Blood Donation Camps

To save lives through voluntary blood donations.

JLG & SHG Awareness

To promote Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) and Self-Help Groups (SHGs) for economic empowerment.

Agriculture and Horticulture Awareness

To enhance agricultural practices and promote horticulture in rural areas.

Road Safety Awareness Programs

To educate communities about the importance of road safety.

COVID-19 Awareness Programs

To ensure rural areas have access to critical COVID-19 information and preventive measures.

Yoga Development Programs

To promote physical and mental well-being through yoga.

Yoga Naturopathy Programs.

To explore natural healing methods for healthier lives.

Cultural Programs

To celebrate the rich cultural heritage of our villages.

Swachh Bharat Awareness

To advocate for a clean and hygienic environment.

Literacy Programs

To enhance literacy rates for a brighter future.

Health Awareness Programs

To foster a culture of good health and well-being.

Educational Awareness Programs

To encourage a thirst for knowledge among rural youth.

National Festivals

To celebrate India's diversity and unity.
